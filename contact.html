Frequently Asked

Do you accept manuscript submissions?

Yes. Purple Squirrel Media works with select authors on editing, formatting, and multi-platform distribution. Send a brief pitch and sample chapter to the email above.

Are your books available outside the US?

Yes. Amazon KDP, Google Play Books, and Apple Books all distribute globally. Kindle editions are available worldwide on day one.

Do you offer bulk or educational pricing?

For bulk orders (10+ copies) or educational licensing, reach out via email and we'll work something out.

Can I translate one of your books?

The Crypto Survival Guide already has translations in Spanish, German, French, Japanese, and Portuguese. For other titles or languages, get in touch.
