About Me

I'm a travel blogger turned author, editor, and publisher. Over the past decade I've visited 70+ countries across six continents, built a readership of 29,000+ people at Expert Vagabond, and turned everything I learned into books.

Today I write about the things I care about most: cryptocurrency security, AI-powered development, and seeing the world on a budget. I also help other authors bring their stories to print through my publishing company.

I believe the best books come from people who've actually done the thing. Every guide I write is tested in the field, not compiled from search results.

Publisher

Purple Squirrel Media

Purple Squirrel Media is an independent publishing house I founded to produce books across crypto, AI, travel, and creative storytelling. We handle the full pipeline: manuscript editing, cover design, formatting, multi-platform distribution, and marketing.

We've published authors including Steven Karsten (military sci-fi), Nick Spanos (Bitcoin Center NYC founder), Teresa Castagnino (design thought leadership), and Scarlett Arana (BitBasel CryptoArt curator).
