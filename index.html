Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I download my eBook?

After purchase, you'll receive a download link via email. For Apple Books, the book will appear in your library automatically. For Kindle, it will be delivered to your registered device.

Are print editions available?

The Crypto Survival Guide and Roger Ramjett are available in paperback on Amazon. Art on the Moon is being prepared as a hardcover art book. More print editions are coming.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds are handled by the platform where you purchased the book (Apple Books, Amazon Kindle, etc.). Please contact their support directly.

Are translations available?

The Crypto Survival Guide is available in Japanese, Portuguese, German, French, and Spanish. The Architecture of Perception is bilingual (Spanish/English). More translations are planned.
